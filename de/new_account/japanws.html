The customer agrees they understand each the following statements regarding OTC binary options trading:

Jede Optionstransaktion hat eine festgelegte Ablaufzeit und ein festgelegtes Ablaufdatum, Entscheidungszeit genannt.

Wenn ein Kunde eine lange Postion hält und sich die Vorhersage während der Entscheidungszeit als falsch herausstellt, dann wird die investierte Kapitalsumme zum Nullwert.

If a customer sells back a long option position before the determination time, then the sale price may be lower than the original purchase price, so there is a possibility of a net loss.

If a customer holds a short position and the prediction is incorrect as of the judgment time, the customer is required to pay the payout amount, and as that will be greater than the trade price that the customer originally received, this will cause a net loss.

Binary.com earns a return from the difference between the total amount of all customers' payment and the total amount of all customers' receipts.

In carrying out OTC binary option trading, a customer needs expert knowledge such as the theoretical value of options, in order to make reasonable investment decisions.
